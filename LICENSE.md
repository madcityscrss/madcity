## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

❌ **Script does not execute after injection**
The executor may be outdated or incompatible with the current **Mad City Script 2026** payload.
**Solution:** Update your executor to the latest version, re-download the `.lua` file, and try again.

❌ **GUI does not appear on screen**
A conflicting script or overlay may be blocking the interface.
**Solution:** Close all other running scripts, reinject, and execute only the **Mad City Script** file.

❌ **All Cars toggle shows locked vehicles**
The unlock sync sometimes misses on a mid-session inject.
**Solution:** Rejoin the server and execute the **Mad City Script All Cars** module on a fresh load.

❌ **Teleportation causes rubber-banding**
Your `teleportDelay` is set too low for the server's tick rate.
**Solution:** Increase the value to `0.5` in your config file and re-execute.

❌ **Auto Farm stops earning cash**
A game update may have shifted NPC or objective positions.
**Solution:** Check for a **Mad City Script 2026** hotfix on the download page or **Mad City Scripts Pastebin**.

❌ **High CPU usage during gameplay**
Running every feature simultaneously can spike resource consumption.
**Solution:** Enable `lowSpecMode` and disable `debugLog` in your configuration block.
